WHO IT'S FOR

Built for everyday commuters.

THE RIDER
The Daily Commuter
Teachers, nurses, office workers, students — anyone who makes the same trip every day. Book a seat, split the cost, arrive on time. No need to own a car or wait for a minibus.
THE DRIVER
Passive Income On Your Way
You're already driving to work. Post your trip, pick up riders along the way, and let the fare offset your fuel costs. No full-time commitment. Just your normal route, paying for itself.
THE CITY
Fewer Cars. Cleaner Roads.
Every shared ride means one fewer car on the road. Less congestion, lower emissions, a cleaner commute for everyone. Twambo isn't just smart — it's the right way to move.
